-- Strumming Open Strings --

Open Strings refer to strings which are played without placing your hand on any the frets

-- Basic (Cowboy) Chords --

The Chords you are about to learn unlocks 90% of music for you.

E Minor: Strum Down while fretting the indicated spots

E Major: Strum Down while fretting the indicated spots

A Major: Strum from the 5th string down while fretting the indicated spots

A Minor: Strum from the 5th string down while fretting the indicated spots

C Chord: Strum down while fretting the indicated spots

G Chord: Strum down while fretting the indicated spots

D Chord: Strum from the 4th string down while fretting the indicated spots
